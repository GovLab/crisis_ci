MODULES INSTRUCTORS PARTNERS CONTRIBUTE A LECTURE

Three Day Evidence Review

By Peter Bragge

In a crisis, we need to know quickly what research information can be useful to aid decision-making - from questions of ‘what works’ to policy feasibility and implementation considerations. In this brief tutorial, Peter Bragge of BehaviourWorks Australia explains principles of approaching the task of rapidly arranging credible research information for policymakers and other leaders.
